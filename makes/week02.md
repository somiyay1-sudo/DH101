This project explores how AI systems reflect biases in their training data. When asked to generate a "Japanese exchange student," the AI initially created a female student, reflecting demographic patterns of international students. Through iterative prompts, I refined the image to better match my actual appearance, revealing both the capabilities and limitations of AI image generation in capturing personal identity.
